********************************QUESTIONS TO ASK DURING INTERVIEWS***********************************



=====================================================================================================
Genuine Questions
=====================================================================================================
-What is the ratio of testers to developers to program managers?
-What is the interaction like?
-What kind of communication methods (technology and processes) are used for project coordination 
    and conflict resolution? 
-How does project planning happen on the team?
-What brought you to this company?
-What has been the most challenging for you?
-Is there any insight you would like to share about the culture of the company and or team I will be working with?



=====================================================================================================
Insightful Questions
=====================================================================================================
- I noticed you use technology x. How do you handle problem y?
- Why did the product choose to use X protocol over Y protocol?
- I know it has benifits like A, B, C, but many companies choose not to use it because of issue D. 



=====================================================================================================
Passion Questions
=====================================================================================================
- I'm very interested in scalability, and I'd love to learn more about it. What opportunites are there
at this company to learn about this?
- I'm not familiar with technology x, but it sounds like a very interesting solution. Could you tell me
a bit more about how it works?
